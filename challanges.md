Challenge 6 - Through the Circle
--------------------------------

Write a program to take a radius of a circle from the user and then calculate the circumfrence and area and display to the user. Do research to find out the formulas for circumfrence and area of circle based on it's radius from the internet.

__Language__: C++ & Python

Example 1:
```
Enter the radius: 5

Circumfrence: 31.42
Area: 78.54
```

Example 2:
```
Enter the radius: 2

Circumfrence: 12.57
Area: 12.57
```
